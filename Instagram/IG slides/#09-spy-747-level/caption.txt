In 2022, the all-time high missed its line by 13 cents. Then the market fell 25%.

The next line up sits at 747. For the first time ever, SPY is trading within 1.3% of it. History says a setup like this resolves one of two ways: a clean breakout higher, or the top of the cycle. At the line, the two look identical.

One signal tells them apart. It cannot be read until the level is touched.

So is 747 the breakout, or the top?
