Hang Seng Index Research

Index-level analysis on the Hang Seng. Long-term structural frameworks for Hong Kong equities.

Start with the market itself, not the companies inside it.

The Hang Seng is the context every Hong Kong position sits in. A stock can be cheap, well run, and still go nowhere for years if the index it trades in is stuck. Regime is the reason. A market compounding off a structural low behaves nothing like one drifting after a break. The same valuation means two different things in those two worlds.

The work here stays at that level: long-term trendlines, cycle structure, and the discount Hong Kong carries against developed markets. What would close that discount, and what would widen it. Know what kind of market you are in before you judge any single name inside it.
