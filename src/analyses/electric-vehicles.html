Electric Vehicles

HKEX-listed EV companies. Regulatory transition cycles, geographic diversification, and multiples that treat volume leaders like commodity producers.

The Chinese electric vehicle market is subject to periodic regulatory transitions that compress volumes and margins across the industry. The market tends to extrapolate these transitions as permanent, re-rating sector leaders at trough multiples. When the transition completes and the underlying demand resumes, the business metrics recover faster than the valuation does. Understanding the regulatory cycle — what caused the volume compression and whether the cause has been resolved — is the central analytical task.
