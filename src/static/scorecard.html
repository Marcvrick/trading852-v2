Methodology

Entry price is the close of the first trading session after the April 10, 2026 publication date — Monday, April 13, 2026 for each ticker. This is the earliest price a reader could realistically transact at after reading the issue.

Last price is the most recent daily close available from Yahoo Finance, fetched live when you open this page.

Stop loss at −10%. If the price drops 10% below entry at any point after publication, the position is closed. The return is locked at −10% and the row is grayed out with the exit date, even if the stock keeps falling.

The scorecard is an honest accountability log, not investment advice. Past performance on a three-figure sample is not a track record.
