Methodology

Entry price is the close of the first trading session strictly after each article's publication date. For a weekend publication, the entry is Monday's opening price instead of the close.

Last price is the most recent daily close available from Yahoo Finance, fetched live when you open this page.

Stop loss at −10%. If the price drops 10% below entry at any point after publication, the line is closed. The return is locked at −10%, the row is highlighted in light red with the exit date, and a small Stopped label appears under the % value, even if the stock keeps falling.

Average return is the simple arithmetic mean of the % change of every line, the Tracker Fund (HSI) excluded. Each ticker counts equally, regardless of price or market cap. The Tracker is shown as a reference only and does not contribute to the average.

The scorecard is an honest accountability log, not investment advice.
